_founded on the idea of making knowledge available to everyone.

For us at tretton37, knowledge and knowledge sharing has always been of utmost importance. Our business model hinges on sharing knowledge, not only with our clients but also with the community and each other. Actively sharing knowledge is a way for us to create additional value in the world around us.

When we work with our clients we actively share our experiences on a daily basis. Not only through the work we do but also through seminars, code-camps, retrospectives, mentoring, and workshops. It is important for us to let our clients have access to our collective knowledge.

_we constantly complement, revise & nurture our knowledge

We have a solid autonomous knowledge sharing strategy which is built on the individual responsibility of actively seeking, using and consuming new approaches and ideas. We entrust an important piece of our organizational resource to all our employees, so that they themselves can decide what and how they want to learn & improve.

Our knowledge foundation is facilitated by regular meet-ups, conferences and interest-groups. Throughout the year, we also organize a couple of internal conferences, called Knowabunga days, which are devoted exclusively to better our selves and our knowledge. Amongst other initiatives we drive our own Technology radar and host Leetspeak, which has been a staple of the Nordic IT community since 2012.

_Leetspeak



The idea of Leetspeak goes beyond a conference, it is a concept built on the love for knowledge. It is an annual gathering for knowledge hungry developers as well as a safe place for the community to meet, share and interact.

We have invented the concept of Leetspeak back in 2012 in Malm√∂, Sweden. It was the first time where developers who were eager to stay at the forefront of High quality software production could be locally challenged by industry leading experts. We started out by wanting to bring inspiring, concrete technical content to the pillars of our community and our bright future stars. Today Leetspeak is a thriving and growing conference with a firm following and high level of desirability.

Community has always been THE cornerstone for tretton37 and Leetspeak is yet another way in which we are able to evolve, strengthen and enrich our valued peers within it.
